Cyber Pulse transforms raw audio data into hypnotic visual experiences. Using advanced FFT analysis and custom shaders, it creates a synesthetic journey that reacts to every beat and frequency.

Optimized for high frame rates, it pushes the boundaries of what is possible with **WebGL** and **Web Audio API** in modern browsers.
