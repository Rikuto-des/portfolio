Aether Lens overlays digital information onto the physical world, creating a seamless blend of reality and data. Users can navigate cities, find hidden gems, and interact with virtual art installations.

The application leverages **SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)** technology to ensure stable and persistent anchoring of digital content in the real environment.
